Under the supervision of Fabian Sinz, I am building deep neural networks to understand how reference frames are encoded by neurons of rhesus macaques under freely-behaving conditions.

Before my PhD, I completed my MSc at the intersection of Riemannian Geometry and Quantum Chemistry at the Federal University of ABC (UFABC), Brazil.
